about me!

I am a Front-End Web Developer with experience building clean, responsive and easily accessible code using HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, JQuery and React. I have 5 years of retail banking background including management of customer service positions which taught me how to work with various levels of staff and clientele while making data-informed operational decisions. Outside of work, I manage a virtual community (with 174 members and growing!) while also taking advantage of all the camping and hiking opportunities my backyard of northern Ontario has to offer.
